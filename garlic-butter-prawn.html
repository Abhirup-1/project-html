Garlic Butter Prawn

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, chicken, and a blend of spices. It is a popular dish in Indian and Pakistani cuisine, known for its rich taste and delightful aroma.

Garlic Butter Prawn

Here's a simple recipe to make delicious Garlic Butter Prawn:

  1. Heat butter in a large skillet over medium heat until melted.
  2. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add prawns to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until they turn pink and opaque.
  4. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and lemon juice over the prawns, stirring well to coat.
  5. Remove from heat and sprinkle fresh parsley on top.
  6. Serve the garlic butter prawns hot with steamed rice or crusty bread.
