About Pot Chickpea Shakshuka

Pot Chickpea Shakshuka is a delicious dish made with various herps & spices, and various other ingredients. It is popular in many cuisines around the world.

Pot Chickpea Shakshuka Recipes

Heat olive oil in a large pot or skillet over medium heat. Add 1 diced onion and cook until translucent. Stir in 3 minced garlic cloves, 1 diced bell pepper, and 1 teaspoon of ground cumin. Cook for another 2-3 minutes. Add 1 can of diced tomatoes and 1 can of drained chickpeas to the pot. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of red pepper flakes. Simmer the mixture for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Create small wells in the sauce and crack eggs into them. Cover the pot and cook until the eggs are set to your liking. Sprinkle with chopped parsley or cilantro, and serve the pot chickpea shakshuka with crusty bread or pita. Enjoy this hearty and flavorful dish.
