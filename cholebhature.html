About Chole Bhature

Pot Chole Bhature is a delicious dish made with various herps & spices, and flour as ingredients. It is popular in many cuisines around the world.

Chole Bhature Recipie

Chole (Chickpea Curry):

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and slit green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  6. Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Cook the masala for a few minutes to enhance the flavors.
  8. Add cooked chickpeas and mix well to coat them with the masala.
  9. Add water as needed to adjust the consistency of the curry.
  10. Cover the pan and simmer for 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  11. Add garam masala and dry mango powder. Stir well.
  12. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, then remove from heat.
  13. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.

Bhature (Fried Bread):
  1. a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and baking soda.
  2. Add yogurt and mix well to form a soft dough.
  3. Knead the dough for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 2-3 hours.
  5. After the resting period, divide the dough into small portions.
  6. Take one portion and roll it out into a circular shape (about 6-7 inches in diameter).
  7. Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadhai over medium-high heat.
  8. Carefully slide the rolled bhature into the hot oil and fry until it puffs up and turns golden brown.
  9. Flip it over and fry the other side as well.
  10. Remove the fried bhature from the oil and place them on a paper towel to remove excess oil.
  11. Repeat the process with the remaining dough portions.
