About Prawn Malai curry

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, chicken, and a blend of spices. It is a popular dish in Indian and Pakistani cuisine, known for its rich taste and delightful aroma.

Prawn Malai curry

Here's a simple recipe to make delicious Prawn Malai curry:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© finely chopped onions, ginger, and garlic until golden brown.
  2. Add ground spices like turmeric, cumin, coriander, and red chili powder, and cook for a minute.
  3. Pour in coconut milk and simmer until the mixture thickens slightly.
  4. Add cleaned and deveined prawns to the pan and cook until they turn pink and are cooked through.
  5. Season with salt and sugar, and add a squeeze of lemon juice for a tangy flavor.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve the delicious Prawn Malai Curry hot with steamed rice or naan bread.
