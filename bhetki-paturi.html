About Bhetki Paturi

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, chicken, and a blend of spices. It is a popular dish in Indian and Pakistani cuisine, known for its rich taste and delightful aroma.

Bhetki Paturi

Here's a simple recipe to make delicious Bhetki Paturi:

  1. Marinate fresh bhetki (Barramundi) fillets with a mixture of turmeric powder, salt, and lemon juice for 15 minutes.
  2. Prepare a paste by grinding together grated coconut, mustard seeds, green chilies, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Place the marinated bhetki fillets on banana leaves and spread the prepared paste evenly on top of each fillet.
  4. Fold the banana leaves to form a packet, securing it with toothpicks or string.
  5. Steam the banana leaf packets in a steamer for about 15-20 minutes until the fish is cooked and the flavors are infused.
  6. Serve the Bhetki Paturi hot, directly in the banana leaf packets, along with steamed rice for a delightful Bengali fish delicacy.
