Vegan Indian Butter Chickpeas

Vegan Indian Butter Chickpeas is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, chicken, and a blend of spices. It is a popular dish in Indian and Pakistani cuisine, known for its rich taste and delightful aroma.

Vegan Indian Butter Chickpeas

Here's a simple recipe to make delicious Vegan Indian Butter Chickpeas:

  1. In a large pan, melt the vegan butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add the minced garlic and ginger paste to the pan. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, garam masala, and cayenne pepper (if using). Mix well and cook for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add the cooked chickpeas to the pan and stir to coat them with the spice mixture. Pour in the coconut milk and season with salt. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend together.
  5. Once the sauce has thickened, remove from heat. Garnish with fresh cilantro.
  6. Serve the vegan Indian butter chickpeas with rice or naan bread for a delicious and satisfying meal.
