about Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a popular Indian dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce. It is known for its rich flavors and smooth texture.

Butter Chicken Recipe

Here's a simple recipe to make delicious Chicken Biryani:

  1. Marinate boneless chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices.
  2. Grill or cook the marinated chicken until cooked through.
  3. In a separate pan, saut√© onions, garlic, and spices until golden brown.
  4. Add tomato puree, cream, butter, and fenugreek leaves to the pan.
  5. Simmer the sauce until it thickens, then add the cooked chicken pieces
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
