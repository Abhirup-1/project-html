About Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, chicken, and a blend of spices. It is a popular dish in Indian and Pakistani cuisine, known for its rich taste and delightful aroma.

Chicken Biryani Recipe

Here's a simple recipe to make delicious Chicken Biryani:

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices.
  2. Soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes and then cook it until partially done.
  3. In a separate pan, layer the partially cooked rice and marinated chicken.
  4. Add saffron milk, fried onions, and ghee to enhance the flavors.
  5. Cover the pan and cook on low heat until the rice and chicken are fully cooked.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
