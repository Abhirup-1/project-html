Vegan chickpea curry

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, chicken, and a blend of spices. It is a popular dish in Indian and Pakistani cuisine, known for its rich taste and delightful aroma.

Vegan chickpea curry

Here's a simple recipe to make delicious Vegan chickpea curry:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat and saut√© the onion and garlic until they become translucent.
  2. Add the cumin, coriander, and turmeric to the pan, stirring well to coat the onion and garlic.
  3. Add the chickpeas, diced tomatoes, and coconut milk to the pan. Stir to combine all the ingredients.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and let the curry simmer for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the vegan chickpea curry over rice or with naan bread, garnished with fresh cilantro.
