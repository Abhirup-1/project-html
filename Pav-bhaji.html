Pav bhaji

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, chicken, and a blend of spices. It is a popular dish in Indian and Pakistani cuisine, known for its rich taste and delightful aroma.

Pav bhaji

Here's a simple recipe to make delicious Pav bhaji:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add finely chopped onions, ginger-garlic paste, and green chilies. Saut√© until onions turn golden brown.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy. Mash the tomatoes to a smooth consistency.
  3. Add pav bhaji masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a minute.
  4. Add boiled and mashed mixed vegetables (such as potatoes, cauliflower, peas, carrots) along with a little water. Mix and cook until the vegetables are well blended and the flavors are infused.
  5. Toast pav (bread rolls) with butter on a griddle until they turn golden brown.
  6. Serve the bhaji (vegetable mixture) hot with a dollop of butter, garnished with chopped onions, coriander leaves, and lemon wedges. Serve with the toasted pav on the side
