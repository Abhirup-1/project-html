Vegan pad thai noodles

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, chicken, and a blend of spices. It is a popular dish in Indian and Pakistani cuisine, known for its rich taste and delightful aroma.

Vegan pad thai noodles

Here's a simple recipe to make delicious Vegan pad thai noodles:

  1. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat and add tofu cubes. Cook until golden brown on all sides.
  3. Add carrots and bean sprouts to the skillet and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until slightly softened.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, peanut butter, lime juice, and brown sugar until well combined.
  5. Pour the sauce over the tofu and vegetable mixture in the skillet, stirring well to coat everything evenly.
  6. Add the cooked rice noodles to the skillet and toss gently until the noodles are coated with the sauce. Serve hot, garnished with chopped peanuts and fresh cilantro. Enjoy your vegan pad Thai noodles!
